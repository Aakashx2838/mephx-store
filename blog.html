How has technology affected my life

Technology, which literally means "machinery and equipment developed from the application of scientific knowledge" is often used with subjects like computers and other branches of information system but in reality tecnology has a broader reach than just dealing with computers. Along with time technology has steadily evolved and still evolving and at a foresight the evolution seems never-ending. The never-ending evolving demeanour has made mankind too dependent of technology. Especially for me, being a student learning to code I spend most of the time around technologies be it software related or hardware related making me heavily dependent upon technology affecting which at times has made my life enjoyable and easy but also stressful and tiring sometimes. The first and foremost impact of technology on my life was with how my ways of learning changed over time. With the existance of internet and multiple plaltforms that deal with providing education on the web. Learning has been easier and reliable a reason to this might be the COVID-19 outbreak which changed people's life in many ways. A few clicks here and there could give a student information worth more that reading a book. For Instance, If I were unable to undestand a specific topic I can search for an alternate explanation coming from a different mentor. Similarly, I spend most of my day watching movies, animes, and playing games which relaxes my mind and adds the experince to new content on the web. I use many technologies such as mobile phones, earphones, tv, etc,. On a daily basis. Communication is important in today's society which is made easier due to modern day technologies I myself spend a decent segment of time communicating with the people around me with the help of technologies.

Just as a coin has two sides technology also comes with the disadvantages. Since it has made me heavily reliable to itself I sometimes end up searching simple commonsensical things on internet. For instance, use of calculators have made me forget simple multiplication or divison. Overuse of technology lowers the creativity of an individual. Not only that overuse of technology can lead to serious physical as well as mental problems. I sometimes overuse computer unknowingly which makes me mentally tired and stressed. In addition to that, overuse of technology causes insomnia hearing audio through headphones for a long time cause ear problems in the future.

To sum up, technology changed our life into a simple and easy situation without any doubt. Technology does a lot to make things in life easier, relaiable, and more efficient. This is so we can do more things in a shorter amount of time, expending the least amount of physical and mental energy possible.
